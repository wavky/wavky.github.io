多多收银隐私政策 | DodorPos Privacy Policy

最后更新 / Last Updated: 2025-02-05

中文版

引言

欢迎使用多多收银（DodorPos）。我们非常重视您的隐私保护。本隐私政策说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。使用本应用即表示您同意本隐私政策中描述的数据处理方式。

应用概述

多多收银是一款轻量级、离线可用的点单与结账应用，专为个体小商户、摊贩、小吃店等场景设计。本应用不需要网络连接即可正常使用核心功能，也不包含实际的支付处理功能。

信息收集与使用

1. 业务数据

本应用会处理以下业务数据：

数据类型 说明 用途
商品信息 商品名称、价格、分类、描述 商品管理与点单功能
商品图片 您添加的商品照片 商品展示
订单记录 交易历史、金额、时间 订单历史查询
报表数据 销售统计、营业汇总 经营分析与报表导出
商铺设置 商店名称、税率、币种配置 个性化商铺设置

数据存储方式：

  • 单机模式：数据仅存储在您的设备本地，不会上传到任何服务器。当您卸载应用或清除应用数据时，这些数据将被永久删除。
  • 云端模式（如启用）：当您选择使用云端同步功能时，上述业务数据将加密传输并存储在我们的安全服务器上，以便跨设备同步和数据备份。您可以随时在应用设置中关闭云端同步功能。

2. 自动收集的数据

为了改进应用质量和用户体验，我们使用以下第三方分析服务：

Firebase Analytics（应用分析）

收集的数据 用途
应用使用统计 了解功能使用情况以改进产品
设备信息 设备型号、操作系统版本
应用版本 追踪版本分布
大致位置 国家/地区级别（基于 IP 地址）

Firebase Crashlytics（崩溃分析）

收集的数据 用途
崩溃日志 识别和修复应用问题
崩溃堆栈跟踪 定位代码问题
设备状态信息 了解崩溃发生时的设备环境

说明：以上自动收集的数据均为匿名数据，不包含任何可直接识别您个人身份的信息。

数据共享与披露

  • 我们不会出售您的个人数据
  • 我们不会与第三方共享可识别个人身份的信息
  • Firebase 服务由 Google 提供，相关数据处理受 Google 隐私政策 约束
  • 我们可能在法律要求的情况下披露信息

应用权限

本应用可能请求以下权限：

权限 用途 是否必需
相机权限 拍摄商品图片、扫描条形码 可选
通知权限 商品库存提醒等通知 可选

用户选择与控制

您可以通过以下方式管理您的数据：

  1. 删除本地数据：在设备设置中清除应用数据，或直接卸载应用
  2. 删除云端数据（如启用）：在应用设置中删除账户，或联系我们请求删除
  3. 关闭云端同步：在应用设置中随时关闭云端同步功能
  4. 限制分析数据收集：在设备设置中限制广告跟踪
  5. 管理权限：在设备设置中随时撤销应用权限

数据保留

  • 本地数据：保留至您主动删除或卸载应用
  • 云端数据（如启用）：保留至您主动删除账户或请求删除数据
  • 分析数据：根据 Google Firebase 的数据保留政策处理（通常为 14 个月）

数据安全

我们采取以下措施保护您的数据：

  • 单机模式下，核心业务数据仅存储在本地，不经过网络传输
  • 云端模式下，使用 HTTPS 加密传输数据，服务器端采用行业标准安全措施
  • 使用 HTTPS 加密传输分析数据
  • 定期更新应用以修复安全漏洞

儿童隐私

本应用不针对 13 岁以下的儿童设计，我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。如果您是家长或监护人，发现您的孩子向我们提供了个人信息，请与我们联系。

隐私政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在应用内或本页面发布，并更新”最后更新”日期。重大变更时，我们会通过应用内通知等方式告知您。继续使用本应用即表示您接受更新后的隐私政策。

联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问、意见或请求，请通过以下方式联系我们：

  • 电子邮箱： contact@wavky.top
  • 开发者：Wavky

我们将在合理的时间内回复您的请求。

English Version

Introduction

Welcome to DodorPos. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using this app, you agree to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

About the App

DodorPos is a lightweight, offline-capable point-of-sale and checkout application designed for small businesses, vendors, and food stalls. The app works without an internet connection for core features and does not include actual payment processing functionality.

Information Collection and Use

1. Business Data

This app processes the following business data:

Data Type Description Purpose
Product Information Product names, prices, categories, descriptions Product management and ordering
Product Images Photos you add for products Product display
Order Records Transaction history, amounts, timestamps Order history lookup
Report Data Sales statistics, business summaries Business analysis and report export
Store Settings Store name, tax rate, currency configuration Personalized store settings

Data Storage Methods:

  • Standalone Mode: Data is stored only on your local device and is never uploaded to any server. When you uninstall the app or clear app data, this data will be permanently deleted.
  • Cloud Mode (if enabled): When you choose to use the cloud sync feature, the above business data will be encrypted in transit and stored on our secure servers for cross-device synchronization and data backup. You can disable cloud sync at any time in the app settings.

2. Automatically Collected Data

To improve app quality and user experience, we use the following third-party analytics services:

Firebase Analytics

Data Collected Purpose
App usage statistics Understand feature usage to improve the product
Device information Device model, OS version
App version Track version distribution
Approximate location Country/region level (based on IP address)

Firebase Crashlytics

Data Collected Purpose
Crash logs Identify and fix app issues
Crash stack traces Locate code problems
Device state information Understand device environment when crashes occur

Note: All automatically collected data is anonymous and does not include any information that directly identifies you personally.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell your personal data
  • We do not share personally identifiable information with third parties
  • Firebase services are provided by Google and are subject to Google’s Privacy Policy
  • We may disclose information when required by law

App Permissions

This app may request the following permissions:

Permission Purpose Required
Camera Capture product photos, scan barcodes/QR codes Optional
Notifications Product inventory alerts and other notifications Optional

User Choices and Controls

You can manage your data through the following methods:

  1. Delete local data: Clear app data in device settings or uninstall the app
  2. Delete cloud data (if enabled): Delete your account in app settings, or contact us to request deletion
  3. Disable cloud sync: Disable cloud sync at any time in app settings
  4. Limit analytics data collection: Limit ad tracking in device settings
  5. Manage permissions: Revoke app permissions at any time in device settings

Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained until you actively delete it or uninstall the app
  • Cloud data (if enabled): Retained until you actively delete your account or request data deletion
  • Analytics data: Handled according to Google Firebase’s data retention policy (typically 14 months)

Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • In standalone mode, core business data is stored locally only and is not transmitted over the network
  • In cloud mode, data is transmitted using HTTPS encryption, and server-side employs industry-standard security measures
  • Analytics data is transmitted using HTTPS encryption
  • Regular app updates to fix security vulnerabilities

Children’s Privacy

This app is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted within the app or on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date. For significant changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications or other means. Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@wavky.top
  • Developer: Wavky

We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.
